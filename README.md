# Plasticflow

Plasticflow is a digital recycling platform that connects individuals, waste collectors, and recycling centers, making plastic collection and recycling easy, accessible, and rewarding.

---

## Overview
Plasticflow helps solve the growing issue of plastic waste pollution in Nigeria by providing an easy way for people to recycle.  
Users can request pickups for their plastic waste, get matched with verified collectors, and ensure plastics are properly delivered to recycling centers, all through a single mobile app.

---

## Why It Matters
Plastic pollution contributes to flooding, drainage blockages, and climate change. Many people want to recycle but lack access to reliable systems.  
Plasticflow bridges this gap, reducing environmental pollution, promoting sustainability, and rewarding responsible waste disposal.

## Key Features
- Pickup Scheduling: Users can request plastic pickups based on their location and availability.  
- Collector Matching: Connects individuals with nearby verified collectors.  
- Recycling Center Integration: Ensures collected plastics are properly processed.  
- Waste Guide: Educates users on recycling best practices and types of recyclable plastics.  
- Transparent Tracking: Track your pickups, deliveries, and recycling progress.

---

## Technologies Used
- Frontend: React Native (mobile app)  
- Backend: Node.js + Express  
- Database: MongoDB (MongoDB Atlas)  
- Hosting: Vercel (Frontend) + Render (Backend)

## Vision
To create a culture of responsible plastic recycling across Africa, where proper waste management is simple, rewarding, and accessible to everyone.
